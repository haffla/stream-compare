Hello!

Stream Compare is a little application that wants to help you decide which music streaming service suits best your musical taste.

There are basically three steps involved

You tell me what you like by importing music from:
  • A music streaming service. Currently supported:
    • Spotify
    • Deezer
    • Napster
    • Rdio
    • LastFm
    • Soundcloud
    • to be extended...
  • An iTunes Library XML file
  • Audio files from your computer (no upload required)
Inspect your imported collection
  • Attach importance to certain artists
  • Mark certain artists to be ignored
  • Then trigger the analysis
View the results of the analysis
  • The following services are analysed
    • Spotify
    • Deezer
    • Napster
    • I hope there will be more
  • See what of your music they offer
  • You might discover new releases
  • See at a glance how they compare
