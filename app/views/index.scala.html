Hello!

Stream Compare is a little application that wants to help you decide which music streaming service suits best your musical taste.

There are basically three steps involved

You tell me what you like by importing music from a music streaming service like Spotify or Soundcloud or by letting me parse your iTunes Library XML file. It is also possible to import audio files directly from your computer (no upload required).

You can inspect your imported music selection, attach importance to certain artists or mark them irrelevant. Then you trigger the analysis.

View the results of the analysis. See what of your music the different streaming services have to offer. You might discover new releases. Currently Spotify, Deezer, Rdio and Napster are part of the analysis. See at a glance how they compare.
